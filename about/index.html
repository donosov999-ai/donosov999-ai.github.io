About PsyGames

A serious, science-based cognitive trainer — not another gimmick app.

PsyGames is 48 validated paradigms from cognitive science, organized into 9 programs for different goals — from biohackers and entrepreneurs to students, seniors and kids. It is built by Denis Onosov (ODV999), an entrepreneur and biohacker, with one principle: real training, honestly presented.

The project started from a simple frustration: most "brain training" apps are dressed-up casual games with bold claims and little science behind them. PsyGames takes the opposite route — every exercise is a task that researchers actually use to measure attention, memory, inhibition, reasoning and processing speed. Nothing is invented for the app store; everything is adapted faithfully from the literature and built to run fast, offline, on every device you own.

Mission

To make rigorous cognitive training accessible to anyone, anywhere — without ads, paywalls or data harvesting. We believe a sharper mind should not depend on a subscription. Train a few minutes a day, track your own progress, and keep your results entirely on your device.

Who builds it

Denis Onosov (ODV999) is an entrepreneur and biohacker based in Yekaterinburg. He builds PsyGames hands-on — selecting the paradigms, translating them across languages and shipping every release for Mac, Windows and Android. The roadmap is driven by one question: does this actually train the brain, or just feel like it does?

Principles

  • Science-based — validated tasks, no inflated promises
  • Privacy-first — no ads, no third-party trackers
  • Works offline — Mac, Windows, Android
  • Available worldwide in 7 languages
